Inviting others to the Map of Co-creators

Dear new co-creator on the Map,

You are warmly welcomed to send and receive invitations, to connect with co-creators and manifest your important, shared life purpose!

Also, please invite your best friends and members of other compassionate organizations to become visible on the Map.

We do, however, ask you to be heart-felt selective, and only invite people with:

  • A unified world view, who don’t feel superior to others because "we are right" and "they are wrong". People with a unified world view will support the group's exploration of compassionate and inclusive solutions.
  • An open mind, without a mission to primarily convert others to "their" solution. Open minded, humble people will allow for synergies and the highest creative potential of the group.
  • A deep yearning for action, and not to only talk. Action oriented people will catalyze the effectiveness of the co-creative team.

    • Thus, please invite all the potential co-creators that you know to join us in transforming our world – people with the personal maturity to co-create, as described in the self-assessment.

    Best regards from

    The Support Team for the "Map of Co-creators"
